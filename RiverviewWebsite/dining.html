Food and Drink Establishments

Take some time to slow down and enjoy the little things.
There are many different types of resturants, bar type places and even a café.


Mannina's Wine House

Stop by for some wine tasting, appetizers, merchandise and even host a private party.




The Anchor Point Bistro

The Anchor Point (TAP) Bistro aspires to be a well-known healthier culinary choice to an excessively overloaded fast food atmosphere.




War Water Brewery

'Wicked Awesome Favorites' and 'Beard Approved' meals with a side of suds, it sure is fan favorite of St Clair.




Drifter's Resturant and Lounge

Beautiful river views and a nice bar location. The meals are delicious. There is a complete bar with a good selection of beer and wine.




London's Ice Cream

&

Ashby's Sterling Ice Cream

Across the street from the lovely municipal park and the boardwalk. They sell the Ashby's brand of ice cream, which comes in a large variety of flavors. You can listen to music inside or outside while sitting around a fire.




St. Clair Coney Island

  Quick, polite, and delicious. Desserts prepared by a skilled baker who mastered her craft years ago. Cheesecakes, brownies, and a variety of other desserts are available.




Subway

A classic among many with fast service, fresh ingredients and tasty sandwiches.




Dragon Wok

Family run business with amazing Chinese food. Dine-in or Take-out, your choice!




Hungry Howies

Classic pizza with great prices and even better taste.
