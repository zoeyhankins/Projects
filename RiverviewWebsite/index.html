The Heart of Downtown St. Clair, Michigan.

The Riverview Plaza has so many different places to vist with a beautiful view of Palmer Park and the St Clair River.
Wander around this outdoor and indoor shopping center to locate specialty stores, relaxed eateries, and an ice cream parlor.
The stores and boutiques provide unique things such as apparel for adults and children, presents, leisure items, and relics.
