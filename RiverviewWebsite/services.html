Get the Care You Need

There are many places in Riverview Plaza that can give you special care.
From dental care to yoga, it is all accessible to you.


Water's Edge Family Dentistry

The patients are the top priority at Water's Edge Family Dentistry. They are interested about your long-term oral health as well as your appearance. Their warm and welcoming team specializes in family dentistry.

Distinctive Eyewear

Distinctive Eyewear is committed to providing the greatest level of optometry service to their patients. They provide a full variety of optometry services, allowing you to meet all of your demands in one location.

Headroom - Another Salon

Very kind, courteous, and professional, with strict adherence to safety regulations. Offering services to meet your hair requirements!

Roots Beauty Bar

Another fantastic full-service salon to visit. Offering all hair services, nail painting, and even lashes. The salon is light, tidy, and inviting, and everyone who works here is extremely courteous.

Norbert Alexander - Hair Design & Day Spa

There are also exquisite services including as pedicures, manicures, and facials, in addition to hair services such as color correction and hair straightening.

Happy Dog Yoga Studio

All of the instructors at Happy Dog Yoga Studio have completed the training and teaching experiences criteria for one or more of the Yoga Alliance designations. You are guaranteed to have a fantastic time with classes, individual sessions, events, and workshops.

Viking Fitness Center

When you visit Viking Fitness Center, you will be able to exercise on a selection of cutting-edge equipment in a hygienic, welcoming, and supportive environment. After that, unwind in their peaceful saunas, washrooms, and individual dressing rooms, all of which are open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
